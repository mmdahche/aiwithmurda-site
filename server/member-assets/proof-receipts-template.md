## Before/After Proof

```text
Before:
- What was slow, broken, missing, or confusing?

Build:
- What did I change?
- Which AI/tool helped?
- What broke during the attempt?

After:
- What works now?
- What metric improved?
- What can I show in 5 seconds?
```
