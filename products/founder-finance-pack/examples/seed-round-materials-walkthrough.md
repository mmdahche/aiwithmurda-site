# Walkthrough — seed round materials chain

Synthetic B2B SaaS preparing a $1.5M seed. Shows how skills chain without
contradicting numbers.

## Starting inputs

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| MRR | $28k (2026-06-30) |
| Net new MRR (June) | +$3.2k |
| Logo churn (June) | 2 of 84 |
| S&M spend (Q2) | $42k |
| New customers (Q2) | 18 |
| Cash | $380k |
| Net burn | $55k/mo |
| Gross margin | 78% |

## Step 1 — saas-metrics-coach

**Ask:** "How healthy are we for seed fundraising?"

Expected agent output:

- ARR ≈ $336k run-rate
- CAC ≈ $2.3k (42k/18); payback ~11 mo at ARPA ~$333/mo × 78% margin
- NRR direction from expansion vs churn (agent asks if missing)
- Verdict: metrics fundable for seed if NRR >100% or churn improving QoQ

## Step 2 — cfo-advisor

**Ask:** "Size the raise and runway post-close."

Expected:

- Target 18 mo × $55k burn ≈ $990k + 15% buffer → **~$1.2–1.5M** range
- Post-close runway date with hire plan stated
- Downside: burn +20% → still >12 mo on $1.5M

## Step 3 — investor-materials

**Ask:** "Build consistency ledger then 12-slide deck outline."

Ledger must show **$336k ARR**, **$1.5M ask**, same customer count everywhere.
Deck ask slide matches use-of-funds buckets tied to milestones.

## Step 4 — investor-outreach

**Ask:** "Cold email to a seed fund that invests in vertical SaaS."

≤150 words, one traction proof with date, link placeholder for deck.

## Step 5 — board-deck-builder (optional pre-board)

If an angel board seat exists: quarterly scorecard using same ledger numbers.

## Receipt

Five skills, one economic story — metrics justify the raise size, materials
match, outreach cites the same proof point.
