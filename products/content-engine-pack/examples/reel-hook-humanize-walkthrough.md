# Walkthrough — reel from hook to humanized caption

Synthetic operator running a solo faceless-adjacent channel about small-team
software shipping. Shows how four of the five skills chain into one
same-day short.

## Starting inputs

- **Topic:** the operator noticed that a well-known dev tool released a
  feature that removes a common workflow step. They subscribe to the tool's
  changelog and saw it today.
- **Primary source:** the tool's public changelog entry, dated today.
- **Corroboration:** two independent posts on a public dev community with
  early takes.
- **Format target:** 30-second vertical short, YouTube Shorts.
- **Voice sample on file:** ~800 words of the operator's past captions.

## Step 1 — trending-content

**Ask:** "Turn this changelog release into six angles across formats."

Skill runs the ethics screen:

- Tragedy? No.
- Authority? Operator ships small-team software daily — earned.
- Recency? Released 4 hours ago. Well within 24-hour window.
- Truth risk? Primary source is the vendor's official channel. Corroborated.
- Piggy-back? Operator has a first-person take (they used the old workflow last
  Tuesday). Passes.

Expected output includes an angle matrix with six labeled angles. The
operator picks the short-form contrarian angle: *"Everyone's celebrating this
release. It's actually going to break how most teams already use the tool."*
Primary source URL noted, on-screen citation planned for 0:00–0:03.

## Step 2 — hooks-angles

**Ask:** "Give me five hook candidates for that contrarian angle."

Expected candidates:

- Candidate 1 (Shock-number): *"3 hours after the release, my whole pipeline broke."* — with a receipt in the script.
- Candidate 2 (Shock-number): *"6 teams I know shipped a workaround today."* — needs corroboration; if not verifiable, reject.
- Candidate 3 (Contradiction): *"Everyone's celebrating. Here's what they missed."* — clean contradiction, receipt required in Action beat.
- Candidate 4 (Contradiction): *"Best release of the year? For half of us it's a rewrite."* — inversion form.
- Candidate 5 (Stakes): *"If you already scripted around the old behavior, this will cost you a weekend."* — proportional stakes.

Operator picks Candidate 3. Skill notes: **receipt required in the Action beat**
— that is, the operator must show what they missed, not just claim it.

## Step 3 — ugc-scriptwriter

**Ask:** "Write a 30-second reel using Candidate 3 as the hook. Solo operator, phone, desk shoot, one screen record cutaway."

Expected paste-ready output:

- **Hook (0:00–0:03):** spoken candidate 3, overlay "What they missed", B-roll = face on frame
- **Problem (0:03–0:10):** old workflow one-liner + why the new default breaks it. Overlay names the specific behavior. B-roll = screen record of the changelog line
- **Action (0:10–0:25):** three sub-beats — (a) the missing behavior, (b) the workaround, (c) the operator's own repo showing the fix. B-roll cues per sub-beat.
- **CTA (0:25–0:30):** one ask — comment WORKAROUND, operator DMs the diff. Overlay = "comment WORKAROUND"

Shot list: 4 shots, all shootable in one session. Overlays block ready to
paste into editor. Caption block ready to paste into upload form.

## Step 4 — content-humanizer

**Ask:** "Rewrite the caption block to match my voice fingerprint from the 800-word sample."

Pass 1 — voice fingerprint from the sample:

- Rhythm: short-short-long. Avg 12 words per sentence.
- Signature words: "shipped", "actually", "obvious in hindsight", "the workaround"
- Banned generic words: "leverage", "unlock", "delve", "unleash"
- Opener habit: opens with a first-person claim
- Closer habit: closes with a one-line question or an open loop

Pass 2 — the caption is rewritten. The diff receipt shows:

```
Generic phrases removed:
  - "In this video we'll explore" → deleted
  - "unlock the potential"        → "actually get"
  - "leverage"                    → "use"

Signature words added:
  - "shipped"           (appears 1×)
  - "obvious in hindsight"   (appears 1×)

Rhythm alignment:
  Original avg sentence:   19 words
  Rewrite avg sentence:    12 words
  Fingerprint target:      12 words
```

## Receipt

Four skills, one shipped short. Trend passed the ethics screen with a
timestamped source. Hook was picked with a specific frame and a required
receipt. Script is shootable in one session with the operator's actual gear.
Caption reads in the operator's own voice, not a generic AI voice, and every
factual claim traces back to the changelog URL or the operator's own repo.

## What this walkthrough does not show

- Actual filming and editing (that's the operator's job, no skill required)
- The `audio-overview` skill — used for the weekly slot, not per-short
- Analytics and iteration (measure what you'd act on, log what the tracker asks for)
- A monetization step — this pack ships execution, not offers
