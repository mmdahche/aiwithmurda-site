# Trending Content

Convert **what people are actually talking about** into publishable angles
with receipts attached.

## When to use

- Daily/weekly content calendar from news or community trends
- Reaction posts to product launches or industry shifts
- "What's happening in [niche]?" briefs

## Workflow

1. **Collect signals** — search, RSS, community threads (minimum 3 sources)
2. **Verify** — cross-check claim appears in ≥2 independent sources OR label single-source
3. **Extract angle** — why YOUR audience cares (not generic hot take)
4. **Draft hook** — use hooks-angles skill; cite trend source in internal notes
5. **Ship checklist** — every stat links to source; remove if unlinkable

## Source citation format

Inline for scripts:
- "Developers on r/X discussed Y (thread, date, engagement level)"
- "Company Z announced Q feature (press release URL)"

Never: "Everyone is saying..."

## Angle types

| Type | When |
|------|------|
| Explainer | New concept audience hasn't heard |
| Operator take | How you'd implement or avoid |
| Myth bust | Common wrong take + evidence |
| Receipt post | Show your own experiment result |

## Ethics

- No manufactured controversy
- No quoting out of context — link primary source
- Satire labeled satire
- Delay post if story still breaking and facts unstable

## Output

```markdown
## Trend brief — [date]
Sources: [bulleted with links]
Verified facts: ...
Audience hook: ...
Recommended format: reel / thread / long post
Claims needing receipts: ...
```
